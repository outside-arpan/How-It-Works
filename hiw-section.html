Base maps

Tern’s proprietary AI is designed to run on third-party base maps. Its Independently Derived Positioning System (IDPS) feeds into location services and integrates with widely used applications such as Google Maps, Waze, OSM, ESRI ArcGIS, and Apple Maps.
